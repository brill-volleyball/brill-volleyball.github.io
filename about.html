About Adam

Adam Brill's journey with volleyball began during his college years, where he not only honed his skills as a player but also met his wife, Shannon. Their shared passion for the sport created a foundation for what would become a volleyball-centered family legacy.

Today, Adam channels his love for volleyball through photography and videography, documenting the athletic journeys of his daughters, Charlotte (15U Black) and Madeline (13U Red), along with their teammates. What started as a way to capture precious moments for his family has evolved into a service that benefits entire teams, coaches, and volleyball families.

With professional equipment and a keen eye for the sport's dynamic moments, Adam attends every game and tournament, positioning himself to capture the perfect serves, digs, sets, and spikes. His commitment goes beyond photography – he often provides livestreams for families who cannot attend in person, ensuring no one misses their athlete's achievements.

Adam's volleyball background gives him unique insight into capturing the most meaningful moments of each match. His technical knowledge of the sport, combined with his photography skills, results in high-quality images and videos that showcase the athletes' talents and the excitement of competitive play.

Photography & Video Equipment

Adam uses professional-grade equipment to ensure the highest quality photos and videos:

  • High-speed DSLR cameras capable of capturing fast-paced volleyball action
  • Professional-grade lenses optimized for indoor sports photography
  • Multiple video cameras for full-match recordings and ball-time highlights
  • Livestreaming equipment to broadcast games for families who cannot attend
  • Post-processing software to enhance and organize photos and videos

All equipment is carefully selected to provide the best possible documentation of the athletes' volleyball journeys, ensuring that every serve, spike, and celebration is captured with clarity.
